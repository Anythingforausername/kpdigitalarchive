Music & Guitar

Updated regularly

Guitar has been my constant companion for the past 5+ years. Started with basic chords and campfire songs, but gradually developed a deeper appreciation for music theory and technique.

I play both acoustic and electric, though I lean towards acoustic for its portability and warmth. Fingerpicking patterns and percussive techniques have become recent obsessions.

Music is meditative for me—a way to disconnect from screens and engage with something purely tactile and auditory. There's always another song to learn, another technique to master.
